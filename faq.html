Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

1. How can I book a flight?
You can book a flight online through our website or mobile app. Simply select your destination, date, and passenger details to complete the booking process.
2. What is the baggage allowance?
The standard baggage allowance for Economy class is 30kg. For Business and First Class, the allowance is 50kg. Additional baggage can be purchased online or at the airport.
3. Can I change my flight?
Yes, you can change your flight up to 48 hours before departure. Any changes to your booking will be subject to availability and applicable fees.
4. What should I do if my flight is delayed?
If your flight is delayed, our team will notify you as soon as possible. We will assist you with rebooking and provide compensation if the delay exceeds certain time limits.
5. How can I check in for my flight?
You can check in online through our website or mobile app 24 hours before your flight's departure. Alternatively, you can check in at the airport at one of our self-check-in kiosks or with a representative.
6. Do you offer special assistance for passengers with reduced mobility?
Yes, we offer special assistance for passengers with reduced mobility. You can request assistance at the time of booking or by contacting our customer service team prior to your flight.
